> Copy this shape when you open your PR against the upstream repo. Keep it short,

**Title:** `docs: fix typo in installation section`

## What
Fixes a typo in `README.md`: "instalation" → "installation" in the Getting
Started section.

## Why
The misspelling appears in the most-read part of the docs and could confuse
newcomers searching the page for "installation".

## Related issue
Closes #142
